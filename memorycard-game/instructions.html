Welcome to the Memory Card Game

Sharpen your brain by matching pairs of cards! Choose a difficulty level, and test your memory skills. Each level increases in difficulty with more cards to match!

Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: 4 Cards (2 Pairs) – A quick warm-up round!
  • Normal: 16 Cards (8 Pairs) – A good challenge for your memory.
  • Hard: 36 Cards (18 Pairs) – Only for the brave!

How to Play

  1. Select a level and start the game.
  2. Click two cards to flip them. If they match, they stay open!
  3. If they don't match, they'll flip back over after a second.
  4. Try to match all pairs with the least moves possible.
